About the project


When I was looking for my end of studies internship, I kept track of all my applications in order to better manage them.
I soon realized I was gathering a descent amount of interesting and relevant data, and decided to produce an infografic to summarize my whole application process.
I love data, and can find things to analyze almost anywhere!

During my internship search, I wanted to challenge my profile and find the best opportunity possible, so I applied to a large range of companies.
Visualizing all this data tells a lot about my personal interests and more widely highlights the job opportunities that I was looking for and considering.
It was also very instructive to see what was effective and identify what could be improved in my job search.

Given the various opportunities that I was offered, I finally decided to join Criteo, the global leader in digital performance advertising, as a Data Analyst Intern!
I'm really pleased to have been recruited by this notorious company, whose business model and key challenges have interested for some time. I'm excited to contribute to its success, and will surely learn a lot within Criteo's renowned innovative environment.

To conclude, I wish to sincerely thank all with whom I have exchanged during my internship search.
Their constructive feedback and precious advice have given me all the tools I need to move forward.
Thanks to them, I'm now ready to begin my professional career with confidence!
